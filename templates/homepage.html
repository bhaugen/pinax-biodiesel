  • Both Topics and Wikis contain articles written by site members.

  • Wiki articles may be formatted to include headings, images, bulleted and numbered lists, and tables, while Topics are unformatted.
  • In general, Topics are suitable for short-lived group discussions, while Wiki articles are suitable for long-lived documents.
